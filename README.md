## Poland (Polish AI Olympiad)

### 1st Edition – 2024

#### Stage I (Online Apr 22–May 27, 2024)
- **Domain:** CV, ML & NLP  
- **Description:** Seven at-home problems: adversarial attacks on CNNs; imbalanced classification; dependency parsing with HerBERT; color quantization; object tracking; pruning; reading-comprehension “riddles.”  
- **Tasks & Resources:**  
  - [Stage I Tasks on GitHub](https://github.com/OlimpiadaAI/I-OlimpiadaAI)

#### Final Stage (Bootcamp Jun 15–21, 2024)
- **Domain:** ML, CV & NLP  
- **Description:** Three timed problems—cipher breaking; out-of-distribution image detection; self-supervised time-series classification—and a machine translation implementation contest.  
- **Tasks & Resources:**  
  - [Final Stage Tasks (branch `final_stage`)](https://github.com/OlimpiadaAI/I-OlimpiadaAI/tree/main/final_stage)

### 2nd Edition – 2025

#### Stage 1 (Online Feb 17–Mar 22, 2025)
- **Domain:** CV & NLP  
- **Description:** Build a coin-counting machine; detect hallucinations in LLM outputs.  
- **Tasks & Resources:**  
  - [Coin Counting Machine](https://github.com/OlimpiadaAI/II-OlimpiadaAI/blob/main/1_etap/1_maszynka_do_liczenia_monet/1_maszynka_do_liczenia_monet.ipynb)  
  - [Hallucination Detection](https://github.com/OlimpiadaAI/II-OlimpiadaAI/blob/main/1_etap/2_wykrywanie_halucynacji/2_wykrywanie_halucynacji_modelowe_rozwiazanie.ipynb)  
  - [Full Stage I repo](https://github.com/OlimpiadaAI/II-OlimpiadaAI/tree/main/1_etap)

#### Stage 2 (Regional Finals Apr 26–27, 2025)
- **Domain:** CV, ML & NLP  
- **Description:** Denoise images & estimate noise parameters; generate credit-scoring explanations; implement GPT-2-based source retrieval.  
- **Tasks & Resources:**  
  - [Non-Normal Distribution](https://github.com/OlimpiadaAI/II-OlimpiadaAI/blob/main/2_etap/rozklad_nienormalny/rozklad_nienormalny.ipynb)  
  - [Credit Borrowing](https://github.com/OlimpiadaAI/II-OlimpiadaAI/blob/main/2_etap/kredytobranie/kredytobranie.ipynb)  
  - [Source Extraction](https://github.com/OlimpiadaAI/II-OlimpiadaAI/tree/main/2_etap/ekstrakcja_zrodel)  
  - [Full Stage II repo](https://github.com/OlimpiadaAI/II-OlimpiadaAI/tree/main/2_etap)
